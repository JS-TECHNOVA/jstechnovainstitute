About JS Technova Institute

At Technova Institute, we empower students from Class 1 to 12 with expert-led online tutoring across all subjects. Aligned with CBSE, UP Board, and ICSE curriculums, we deliver personalized education that builds confidence, strengthens concepts, and ensures academic excellence — all from the comfort of home.

Who We Are

Technova Institute is more than just an online education platform—it's a vision to transform the way students learn, grow, and succeed in today's digital age. We believe in nurturing not just academically competent individuals but confident, curious, and self-driven learners. At Technova, we offer a personalized learning experience for students from classes 1 to 12 across all major boards including CBSE, ICSE, and UP Board, with comprehensive coverage of all core subjects like Mathematics, Science, English, Social Studies, and more. What sets us apart is our student-centric approach—each learner is unique, and so is their learning journey. That's why we emphasize customized mentorship, live interactive sessions, regular doubt-solving, and conceptual clarity through real-world examples.
